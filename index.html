About the Event

Night Wings is a nighttime aviation photography event where passionate individuals gather under the starry night sky to capture stunning images of airplanes. The event takes place at an airport and includes helpful tips for nighttime photography, opportunities to connect with other enthusiasts, and a chance to share stories and photos. Guests are encouraged to bring their own equipment, such as zoom lenses and tripods, to enjoy the creative and calm atmosphere filled with the sounds and sights of airplanes.

What to Expect

Experience expert photography tips, explore interactive aircraft displays, and connect with a vibrant community of aviation enthusiasts, all while enjoying the calm and inspiring atmosphere of the night sky.

Photography Tips

Learn techniques for capturing airplanes at night with guidance from experts.

Community

Connect with fellow aviation and photography enthusiasts to share ideas and experiences.

Creative Atmosphere

Enjoy the calm, inspiring environment while capturing beautiful nighttime shots.
