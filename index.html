About the Event

Night Wings is a nighttime aviation photography event where passionate individuals gather under the starry night sky to capture stunning images of airplanes. The event takes place at an airport and includes helpful tips for nighttime photography, opportunities to connect with other enthusiasts, and a chance to share stories and photos. Guests are encouraged to bring their own equipment, such as zoom lenses and tripods, to enjoy the creative and calm atmosphere filled with the sounds and sights of airplanes.
